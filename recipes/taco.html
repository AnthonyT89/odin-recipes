Taco

This is a descriptive paragraph descripting the taco and the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Meat
  • Onion Cilantro mix

Steps

  1. Warm up tortilla.
  2. Throw cooked meat in tortilla with onion cilantro mix.
  3. Fold it like a taco and shove it in your mouth.
