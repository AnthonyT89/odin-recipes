Quesadilla

This is a cheesey quesadilla. Kids like them, grownups love them.

This is a paragraph describing how dangerously cheesey a quesadilla can be. Pragraphs don't have automatic line breaks.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put butter on tortilla and throw in pan.
  2. Shred cheese on top of tortilla.
  3. Fold half of tortilla in over cheese.
  4. Flip after cheese has melted to crunch up the top side.
